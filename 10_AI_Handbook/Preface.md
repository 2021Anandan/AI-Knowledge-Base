\# Preface



Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming education by enabling educators to create engaging, personalized, and efficient learning experiences. From lesson planning and content generation to assessment and research, AI has become an essential tool for modern teaching.



This handbook, \*\*The AI-Ready Educator: Practical Tools for Smart Teaching Using Artificial Intelligence\*\*, has been developed to help faculty members, teachers, researchers, trainers, and higher education professionals understand and effectively use AI in academic environments.



The handbook combines theoretical concepts with practical examples, covering topics such as Generative AI, Prompt Engineering, GitHub, Local AI Models, AI-assisted assessment, responsible AI practices, and emerging trends in education. Each chapter is designed to provide clear explanations, real-world applications, and hands-on guidance.



Rather than replacing educators, AI serves as a powerful assistant that enhances creativity, productivity, and student engagement. By adopting AI responsibly, educators can prepare learners for the challenges and opportunities of an AI-driven future.



It is hoped that this handbook will serve as a valuable reference for teaching, learning, research, faculty development programmes, and self-study.
