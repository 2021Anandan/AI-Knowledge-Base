This handbook is a comprehensive guide for educators who wish to integrate Artificial Intelligence into teaching, learning, assessment, research, and academic administration.



It is designed for:



\- 👨‍🏫 Faculty Members

\- 🎓 Engineering Educators

\- 📚 Teachers

\- 🧑‍🔬 Researchers

\- 💻 AI Enthusiasts

\- 🏫 Trainers
